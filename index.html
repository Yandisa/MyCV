About Me

From Gears to Code

With over a decade of experience maintaining critical systems in aviation and pharmaceuticals, I've mastered the art of mechanical problem-solving. Now, as a HyperionDev student, I'm translating that expertise into software engineering. My journey from torque specifications to Python syntax gives me a unique perspective on building reliable systems - whether they're physical or digital.

While I'm currently immersed in coding bootcamp challenges, my maintenance background remains my superpower. Those 10+ years taught me systematic debugging, precision documentation, and how to thrive under pressure - skills that directly translate to crafting clean, maintainable code. I'm particularly passionate about developing applications that solve tangible engineering problems.

My next mission? Developing Predictive Maintenance Web App, Maintenance Tracker Application, and Smart Inventory Management System that leverage both my mechanical expertise and new coding skills. The future of engineering is interdisciplinary, and I'm committed to being bilingual in its two most crucial languages: machinery and code.
